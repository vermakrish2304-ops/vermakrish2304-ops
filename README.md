## 🧠 About Me

I'm a pre-final year Electronics & Communication Engineering
student at **Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra**, specializing
in **VLSI and RTL Design**.

I work at the intersection of software and silicon — writing
Verilog that eventually becomes real hardware. My focus is on
understanding chip design from the ground up: from RTL
description all the way through synthesis, physical design, and
layout.

Currently building expertise across the complete **RTL-to-GDSII**
VLSI design flow using industry-standard tools.
